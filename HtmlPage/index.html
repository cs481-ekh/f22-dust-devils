Background

Dust devils are a dominant source of dust in the martian atmosphere. Datasets from previous missions have revealed the dust devils structures and elucidated their dust-lifting capacities. On the Mars 2020 rover, there is a uniquely sensitive and capable suite of meterological instrumentation (Mars Environmental Dynamics Analyzer) or MEDA for short. MEDA has combined this data to reveal the structures of martian dust devils.
